Recommended Diet Plan

  • Avoid fried food.
  • Use 2-3 tbsp of cooking oil /day.
  • Avoid saturated fats like butter, dalda.
  • Avoid mutton,beef,egg yolk.
  • Can have chicken, fish cooked in dry heat like roasted or steamed.
  • Have pulses 2 bowls /day.
  • Avoid white rice, maida products and white bread.
  • Avoid potato, arbi, sweet potato, beetroot.
  • Avoid banana, litchi, cheeko, grapes, dates, raisins.
  • Can have apples, papaya, guava, pear, orange.
  • Avoid sweets, desserts and cold drinks.
  • Take a low salt diet.
